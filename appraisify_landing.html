Features

Everything you need for a complete appraisal cycle

No external tools. No spreadsheets. Everything runs natively inside your Bitrix24 portal.

📋

Structured 3-Phase Workflow

Self-assessment → Reviewer evaluation → Partner review. Each phase is gated — reviewers only unlock once the reviewee submits.

🚀

Bulk Appraisal Launch

Select multiple employees and launch all their appraisal cycles at once. Configure once, apply to all.

📊

CRM Deal Tracking

Every appraisal is tracked as a CRM deal with a dedicated Kanban board for at-a-glance progress monitoring.

📄

PDF Report Generation

Download a polished PDF report for any completed appraisal — ready to share with the employee or archive to HR records.

🛠️

Custom Question Templates

Build and reuse your own question sets with scored questions and open-text fields tailored to your review criteria.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Employees see only their own appraisal. Reviewers see only their assigned cases. Admins have full visibility.

Getting Started

Up and running in minutes

One-click install from Bitrix24 Market. No configuration needed — Appraisify sets itself up automatically.

1

Install from Bitrix24 Market

Find Appraisify in the marketplace and click Install. The app opens automatically inside your portal.

2

Complete the Setup Wizard

The admin sees a one-time welcome wizard. Appraisify auto-creates your CRM pipeline, stages, and custom fields.

3

Launch Your First Appraisal

Select employees, choose a question template, assign a reviewer and partner, and click Launch.

4

Track, Review & Report

Monitor progress on the Kanban board. Download PDF reports when cycles complete.

💡 Also on Bitrix24

On a Professional plan or above? Try Appraizzie.

Both Appraisify and Appraizzie are powered by Bitrix24 — the difference is how they integrate. Appraisify uses CRM deals, which makes it accessible to Standard plan users and above. Appraizzie uses Bitrix24's SPA framework for a more embedded experience, but requires a Professional plan or above. We're continuing to improve Appraisify to support all workflows — but if you're on Professional and prefer the SPA approach, Appraizzie is available now.
