admin_panel_settings Admin

Manages the full cycle: selects employees, assigns reviewers/partners, creates question templates, and views all results.

person Employee

Fills out their self-assessment. Cannot see reviewer or partner scores until the cycle is complete.

supervisor_account Reviewer

Evaluates assigned employees after their self-assessment is submitted. Sees the employee's scores for context.

handshake Partner

Provides a peer perspective after the reviewer phase. Sees both previous scores. Finalises the appraisal cycle.
