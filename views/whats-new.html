AI Template Setup Wizard

New ✨ Powered by Gemini

Generate a complete suite of appraisal templates tailored to your company in one session

Skip the blank-page problem. The AI Consultant asks about your company — industry, teams, roles, and review types — then generates a full suite of tailored appraisal templates in one go. Each template comes with scope questions, engagement questions, and 1–5 scoring guidance.

  • check_circle Multi-turn chat — AI asks 2–3 questions per round to understand your company before generating anything
  • check_circle Plan first — AI proposes a list of templates and waits for your approval before generating
  • check_circle Preview before saving — review every question in each template before committing to your library
  • check_circle Up to 5 templates per session — start a new session anytime to generate more

AI Question Assist

New ✨ Powered by Gemini

A built-in AI chat panel inside the Question Builder for generating and refining questions

A built-in AI chat panel inside the Question Builder. Describe what you need — the AI suggests questions in context, grouped by section, based on the template type, team, and role you're working on. Add them one by one or refine individual questions with a focused Improve mode.

  • check_circle Context-aware — AI knows the template type, team, and role you're building for
  • check_circle One-click add — add any suggested question directly to your template without copy-pasting
  • check_circle Improve mode — click Improve on any question to open a focused chat that refines it in place
