## Content Strategy to Capture PIP Malaysia Keywords

Rather than cramming keywords onto your product page, the best approach is to create a small content hub around PIP Malaysia. Here's a suggested structure:

### Page: `/appraisify/pip-malaysia/`
**Target keyword:** "PIP Malaysia", "performance improvement plan Malaysia"

A dedicated landing page explaining:
- What a PIP is in the Malaysian context (Employment Act 1955)
- How Appraisify helps HR managers run a legally compliant PIP process
- Call to action: try Appraisify free

### Blog Post 1: `/blog/performance-improvement-plan-malaysia-guide/`
**Target keyword:** "performance improvement plan Malaysia", "how to write a PIP in Malaysia"

A comprehensive guide: what is a PIP, legal requirements, step-by-step process, free template. End with: "Manage your PIP entirely inside Bitrix24 with Appraisify."

### Blog Post 2: `/blog/pip-software-malaysia/`
**Target keyword:** "PIP software Malaysia", "PIP tracking software Malaysia"

Explain why Malaysian HR teams need software (not just Word docs) to manage PIPs properly, why paper trails matter for Industrial Court cases, and how Appraisify solves this.

### Blog Post 3: `/blog/underperforming-employee-malaysia/`
**Target keyword:** "underperforming employee Malaysia", "how to manage underperforming employee Malaysia"

Top-of-funnel content catching HR managers before they even know what a PIP is.
